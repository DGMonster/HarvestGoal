# HarvestGoal

HarvestGoal is a lightweight farming progress tracker for **World of Warcraft**.

Track your farming targets directly in-game by assigning items to customizable slots, setting collection goals, and monitoring your progress in real time.

The addon is designed to stay **simple, lightweight, and easy to use**, while helping players keep track of farming materials, crafting reagents, or other collectible items.

---

## ✨ Features

• Add items via **drag & drop**  
• Set **custom goal amounts** for each slot  
• Real-time tracking of items in **bags and bank**  
• **Horizontal or vertical layout** options  
• **Movable and lockable** window  
• **Minimap button** with quick access menu  
• Clean custom dropdown menu  
• **Lightweight and performance-friendly**

---

## 🎯 How It Works

1. Drag an item onto one of the tracking slots  
2. Right-click the slot to set your farming goal  
3. Collect items and watch your progress update automatically  

HarvestGoal continuously tracks the item count in your **bags and bank**, updating the progress as you collect materials.

---

## ⚙️ Commands

`/hg`  
`/harvestgoal`

Toggles the HarvestGoal window.

## 💡 Inspiration

The idea for HarvestGoal was inspired by the addon **FarmIt**, with the goal of creating a **lightweight and flexible item farming tracker** with a modern and customizable interface.
